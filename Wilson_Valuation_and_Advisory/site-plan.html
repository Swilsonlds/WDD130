Overview

Purpose

My Father started his own real-estate appraisal business three years ago. Up until this point, his business has grown almost exclusively by word of mouth. I would like to create a draft of a website for my dad’s business. The primary purpose of the website will be to extend the “reach” of the business beyond just word of mouth. The secondary purpose of site will be to facilitate communication between my dad and his clients by providing clients with multiple means communication.

Audience

Current and prospective customers of my dad's business

Home page

What is a Commercial Real Estate Appraiser?

A real-estate appraiser is an individual who is trained to assess the market value of properties, and create detailed reports that justify their valuations. A commercial real estate appraiser is one who specializes inspecting, and assessing the value of commercial properties.

Why should I choose WVA?

Wilson Valuation and Advisory is a locally owned and independently run appraisal company that specializes in the appraisal of apartment complexes. Our team has over 20 years of experience working with industry leading companies. Our team has received all necessary licenses and permits to perform appraisals throughout western Washington, and the pacific north-west.
